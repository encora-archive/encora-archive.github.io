What To Do (Without Broadway!): A Day-to-Day Schedule

Source: Broadway.com | Posted: October 1 2020 @ 10:02 am

Even with Broadway on hold, there's plenty to do. Trust the Broadway.com editors to get you in on the best online events, theater-centric films, TV shows and more!

THURSDAY, OCTOBER 1
Sweeney Todd Cast Reunion on Stars in the House @ 8PM ET
Len Cariou, Victor Garber, Sarah Rice and Ken Jennings reunite remotely to discuss their time in Stephen Sondheim and Hugh Wheeler's bloody good musical.
Watch Online

FRIDAY, OCTOBER 2
Tonight at the London Coliseum: Cassidy Janson @ TBD
A musical theater powerhouse, most recently seen in an Olivier Award-nominated as Anne Hathaway in the pop musical & Juliet, live-streamed in concert from the iconic U.K. stage.
Tickets are £15. More Info

Freestyle Love Supreme Academy Showcase @ 8PM ET
Freestyle Love Supreme, the critically acclaimed troupe founded by Thomas Kail, Lin-Manuel Miranda and Anthony Veneziale, is offering virtual fall classes and events, kicking off with a lively showcase. An educational hip-hop weekend for kids and intro courses for adults are available.
