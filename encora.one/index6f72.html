Watch Now: David Byrne’s American Utopia Official Trailer

Source: BroadwayDirect.com | Posted: September 22 2020 @ 3:06 pm

David Byrne’s American Utopia brings the critically acclaimed Broadway show to HBO in a one-of-a-kind film directed by Oscar and Emmy-winner Spike Lee. Recorded during its run at Broadway’s Hudson Theatre in New York City, David Byrne is joined by an ensemble of 11 musicians, singers, and dancers from around the globe, inviting audiences into a joyous dreamworld where human connection, self-evolution, and social justice are paramount. Stream David Byrne's American Utopia Saturday, October 17 on HBO Max.

What To Do (Without Broadway!): A Day-to-Day Schedule

Source: Broadway.com | Posted: September 22 2020 @ 2:45 pm

Even with Broadway on hold, there's plenty to do. Trust the Broadway.com editors to get you in on the best online events, theater-centric films, TV shows and more!

TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 22
Broadway Buskers @ 7PM ET
Times Square’s annual concert series celebrating the singer-songwriters of the theater community moves to a virtual format. Eisa Davis, Will Van Dyke, Tidtaya Sinutoke and Isabella Dawis are set to perform. Donations can be made to BC/EFA's COVID-19 Emergency Assistance Fund or to the Broadway Advocacy Coalition.
Watch Online

TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 22
Belfast Blues @ 7PM ET
The Irish Rep kicks off its digital season with Geraldine Hughes performing her autobiographical solo play, which was filmed at the Lyric Theatre in Belfast, Ireland.
Watch Online

TUESDAY, SEPTEMBER 22
What the Hell Is A Republic Anyway? Begins @ 7PM ET
As part of New York Theatre Workshop's Artistic Instigator project series, Tony winner Denis O'Hare and Lisa Peterson dive deep into this history of the Roman Republic and expose their own process, demonstrating the difficulties around collaboration.
